About the event

Built by hackers, for hackers.

OVERCLOCK started three years ago as a dorm-room idea and now pulls in students from across the region for one weekend of caffeine, code, and chaos. No lectures, no fluff — just 36 hours to take an idea from sketch to demo.

Teams of up to 4 get mentor support, free food, and a stage to demo in front of real judges from industry. First-timers welcome — half our hackers show up with zero prior hackathon experience.

TRACK 01

AI / ML

Build anything that learns, predicts, or generates. Bring your own models or fine-tune on the spot.

  • API credits provided (OpenAI, Anthropic, HuggingFace)
  • Mentor office hours every 4 hours
  • Bonus prize for best use of open-source models

Good to know

FAQ

Do I need a team before I arrive?+
Nope — team formation happens during the opening ceremony if you're solo. Teams can be 1–4 people.
Is there a cost to attend?+
Free for all registered students. Food, swag, and workspace are covered by sponsors.
What should I bring?+
Laptop, charger, student ID, and anything hardware-track hackers want to tinker with.
Can first-timers join?+
Absolutely — about half our hackers each year are attending their first hackathon ever.
